Theodore is a half-orc member of [[The Bandit Bashers]]. Throughout the [[ChroNicles]] campaign Theodore displayed genius tactics throughout numerous encounters and found countless solutions to the various challenges faced by the bandit bashers. 

Since the defeat of [[Garsooth]] Theodore has continued to live in the woods of [[Hen Ymorda]], often causing chaos and sending special packages to people throughout the region.
