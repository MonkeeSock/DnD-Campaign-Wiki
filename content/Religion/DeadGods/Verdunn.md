Domains: Tempest

Symbol: A swirling spiral

Bio: Verdunn was a God of water and storms, but was killed during [[The War of the Gods]] by [[Azurlee]]. It is believed that his body lies at the bottom of Llyn Lake in [[Wynion]] which is believed to have infinite depth. Sometimes sailors can see a titanic hand reaching up from the depths of the lake as they sail above it.Â 

Worship: Worship of Verdunn is rare in the current age, but he is sometimes worshiped by sailors. They will often pray to him for calm weather and smooth sailing.
